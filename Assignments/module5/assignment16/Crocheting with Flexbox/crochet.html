Crocheting 101

Information from WikiHow and Sewing.com

Activity:

Crochet

Tools Needed:

Few

Skill level:

Easy

Tools and Supplies

  • Yarn
  • Crochet Hooks
  • Scissors
  • Darning Needle
  • Tape Measure
  • Hook Organize
  • Stitch Markers
  • Row Counter / Digital Row Counter
  • Stitch Patterns
  • Crochet Book
  • Crochet Materials Organizer

Directions

  1. Make a test swatch.
  2. Crochet a chain.
  3. Make a slip stitch (abbreviated sl st.) This stitch is used to join work, bind off stitches, reinforce edges or carry yarn to a different working position without adding extra height.
  4. Make a single crochet (sc).
  5. Double crochet. Double crochet stitches are great for making sweaters and scarves because they are a bit looser than other stitches (so your sweaters will be more comfy.)
